1. Aesthetic Philosophy: "Brutalist Editorial"The design is a collision between Swiss Minimalism and Digital Brutalism.High Contrast: Deep black (#050505) backgrounds paired with pure white typography and a surgical neon accent (#00ff88).Tactile Texture: An invisible "Analog Noise" overlay (opacity: 0.05) breaks the digital perfection, giving the screen a paper-like or film-grain feel.Kinetic Energy: The site is never static. Every scroll and hover triggers a micro-interaction (Scramble, Lighting, or Rotation).2. Typography & Visual HierarchyThe site utilizes a "Serif-Primary" layout to evoke the feeling of a high-end fashion magazine.Font RoleFont FamilyStyleHeadersQuincy CFBold, tight tracking, high-impact.AccentsPlayfair DisplayItalicized, elegant, used for sub-headers and bios.TechnicalMonospaceSmall caps, spaced-out tracking (0.3em), used for labels.3. Interaction Design (The 5 Pillars)I. The Scramble / Decoding EffectLogic: Upon entering the viewport or hovering, text strings iterate through random alphanumeric characters before stabilizing.Purpose: Communicates the "CS/Engineering" identity through a digital glitch aesthetic.II. Scroll-Linked "Lighting"Logic: As bio paragraphs reach the center 40% of the viewport, their opacity transitions from 0.1 to 1.0.Purpose: Focuses the user’s attention on one line of text at a time, creating a "spotlight" narrative.III. Horizontal Temporal PinningLogic: The vertical scroll locks at the "Experience" section. The gsap.pin function translates the timeline container horizontally across the X-axis.Purpose: Breaks the monotony of vertical scrolling and creates a cinematic "timeline" feel.IV. Interactive Project WallLogic: An accordion-style list using AnimatePresence.Interaction: Clicking a project expands a description and shifts the sibling elements. Hovering triggers a title scramble.V. Progress DynamicsLogic: A fixed-position vertical bar on the right tracking scrollYProgress.Purpose: Provides a constant visual anchor of the user's location within the "story."4. Section BreakdownHeader & NavigationAvailability Badge: A fixed "AVAIL. 2026" pill signals professional readiness.Logo: Minimalist text-based logo using mix-blend-difference to remain visible over any background.Hero SectionImage Treatment: Grayscale, high-contrast images with a bottom-heavy gradient fade into the background.Tagline: "Be creative — Be you" acts as the core personal brand statement.Footer & ContactKinetic Element: A rotating dashed "Stay Curious" badge adds constant movement.Social Dock: Circular, responsive buttons that invert colors on hover to provide high-contrast feedback.5. Technical Implementation DetailsFramework: React 18+Animation Engine: GSAP (ScrollTrigger) + Framer Motion.Styling: Tailwind CSS (Utility-first Brutalism).Performance: Image grayscale filters and noise overlays are CSS-driven to ensure high FPS during scroll-linked animations.
